\section{Limitations}
\label{sec:limitations}

% Address subjective scoring, selection into championships, changing sheets,
% unequal schedules, missing membership/staff/show-completion variables,
% pandemic discontinuity, non-random judge assignment, and SCPA-only scope.
The analysis cannot observe performer membership, staff continuity, rehearsal
time, budget, repertoire difficulty, or show completion. Its findings are
predictive associations and should not be interpreted causally.

Competitive indoor percussion combines athletic execution, musical
performance, and visual design. Its scores are expert judgments rather than
direct physical measurements, and ensembles are expected to improve as a
season progresses. This creates a useful forecasting question: how much of a
group's eventual competitive outcome is already visible at its debut?
